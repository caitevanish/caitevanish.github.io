About Me

I’m an Arizona native currently living in the Bay Area. I was raised near Phoenix and I am a member of the Navajo Nation. I graduated from the University of Arizona in Tucson, AZ with an Art History degree and a 2D Studio Art minor.
With 5+ years of experience working as a muralist, an assistant and collaborator with artists, graphic designers, and freelancers, co-creating with other creative entrepreneurs revealed to me that I enjoyed making the creative process streamlined and automated.
It eventually came to my attention that the ﬁne arts was no longer my long term career direction. It took the 2020 COVID-19 pandemic for me to see that my eye for detail and my analytical, problem solving mind could be quenched by the challenges of web development and programming.
In 2022 I graduated from devCodeCamp (a full stack development coding bootcamp) and within a year I saw many of my dream projects become full-ﬂedged programs.
